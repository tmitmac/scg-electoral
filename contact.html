Common Questions

Many answers can be found in our frequently asked questions section before contacting us.

You can report any concerns about election procedures or irregularities by contacting our hotline at (123) 456-7891 or by filing a formal complaint through our online system. All reports are investigated thoroughly.

Complaints about candidates should be directed to the Square Country Ethics Commission. However, complaints regarding campaign finance violations can be filed with our office.

Job openings are posted on the Square Country government jobs website. We also recruit temporary election workers before major elections - contact your local election office for these opportunities.

Many datasets are available for download on our website. For custom data requests, please complete the Research Request Form available in our Data Downloads section.
