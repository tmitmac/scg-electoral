About the Electoral Commission

Learn about our mission, values, and the team dedicated to ensuring fair elections in Square Country.

Our Mission

The Square Country Electoral Commission is an independent body established to administer free and fair elections at all levels of government. Our mission is to ensure that every eligible citizen can exercise their democratic right to vote.

We oversee the electoral process from voter registration to the announcement of results, working to maintain public confidence in the integrity of our elections.

Our Team

The Commission comprises seven members appointed through a bipartisan process to ensure balanced representation. Our staff includes election experts, legal advisors, and administrative professionals.

We work closely with local election officials across Square Country to implement consistent standards and procedures throughout all voting districts.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We conduct all electoral processes with the highest ethical standards and transparency.

Inclusivity

We strive to make voting accessible to all eligible citizens regardless of background or ability.

Excellence

We continually improve our processes and adopt best practices in election administration.

Our History

1985

The Square Country Electoral Commission was established by constitutional amendment to oversee all levels of elections.

1992

Implemented the first computerized voter registration system nationwide.

2004

Introduced early voting options to increase voter participation.

2020

Launched online voter registration and mobile voting information apps, and online voting.
